Help
The EZ-B v4 accepts Wi-Fi connections from other devices when it is configured as an Access Point.

For example, you would configure the EZ-B v4 to operate in Access Point mode to connect directly to it from another device (i.e. Computer, iPad, iPhone, Android Device, etc.). When you connect a device to the EZ-B v4 in Access Point mode, you will no longer have access to the internet. In order to have access to the internet from your device while connected to the EZ-B v4 in Acess Point Mode, you will require two Wi-Fi adapters on your device.
