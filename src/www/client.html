Configure the Client Mode Settings to join your EZ-B to an existing Wi-Fi network.

If you already have a Wi-Fi network, you may enter the network name and password to join the EZ-B v4 to it. This will make your EZ-B v4 a client of your existing Wi-Fi network. When you connect the EZ-B v4 to your Wi-Fi network, you may not know the IP Address that it was assigned. EZ-Builder includes an EZ-B v4 scanner which will search and find all EZ-B v4's on your network.
